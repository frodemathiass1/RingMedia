Supported Media Services

Netflix is an online media service that offers a big catalogue of movies and series to subscribing customers. With great success the recent years, they have become one of the largest players on the market who also produce and air own in house productions such as the very successful TV-series House of Cards.

HBO Nordic is a video-on-demand and scheduled TV channel in Scandinavia operating under the American broadcaster with the same name. HBO is the longest runner on the Pay-TV market and offers an enormous catalogue of exclusive productions, ranging from the successful series such as The Sopranos   to the more recent Game of Thrones and Boardwalk Empire to name a few.

C More Entertainment operates under Canal Digital and is a pay TV company as well as an internet service provider. Live streaming of sports and entertainment are primarily their focus but also offers a wide range of films, TV series and entertainment.

Viaplay is the online streaming service for the TV company Viaplay. Access to Viaplay is included in Viasat subscriptions but also available as a standalone subscription. Viaplay is the biggest distributor of live sports events in Scandinavia and offers a wide range of TV series and productions.

NRK Nett-TV is the free online TV solution for the Norwegian Broadcasting Corporation NRK Live TV streaming is available as well as previously aired media.

TV 2 Sumo is a Web TV service from TV2. Sumo provides online access to sports, movies and broadcasting content. TV2 is one of the largest sport content providers with exclusive rights to live stream the English Premier League and offers subscription plans suited for desired needs.

Dplay is the web solution for Discovery Networks Norway DNN is a television group and offers a series of TV channels such as TV Norge, Max and Fem to name a few. Dplay is their web solution with exclusive content available online

Apple Music is the music streaming service developed by Apple Incorporated. Apple revolutionized the digital music world with Ipod and Itunes. Apple Music this is their take on online music and radio streaming. Apple Music offers a free trial month for new customers.

Spotifyis by far the most popular and largest commercial music streaming and podcast services on the market with over 20 million paying subscribers . Spotify provides digital rights management-restricted content, free for all user but also offers ad free subscriptions as well as premium subscriptions to suit desired needs.

WiMP, or Tidal as they are currently changing their name to, is a Norwegian music streaming service developed by the music shop Platekompaniet and Aspiro. The service is available on mobiles, tablets and most network players as well as computers.

Google Play Music is a music streaming service operated by. Google. Users can also buy music tracks from Google Play music without a subscription. Standard subscriptions is available at no cost, as well as monthly fee subscriptions with access to for instance creating custom radio-stations or storing music for listening offline.
